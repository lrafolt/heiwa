Seminars led by Leo Rafolt.

Nanbudo dojo Heiwa is open to practitioners of all ages, from the youngest, kindergarten age (for whom a special Nanbudo group is provided), to mature recreational Nanbudoka, who adapt the art to their own needs.
Groups are divided according to age and degrees, which is why learning in the Dojo is almost individual.

Membership in Nanbudo dojo Heiwa includes the following:

  • - regular payment of the monthly membership fee, which is determined at the beginning of the season;
  • - regular attendance of training, where the trainees create their own schedule;
  • - participation in public presentations of Nanbudo, if desired;
  • - participation and practice in national and international seminars, if desired, etc.
